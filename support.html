Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set my sobriety date?

When you first open Sobriety Score, the onboarding flow will ask you to set your sobriety start date and choose what you're tracking. You can update this at any time in Settings.

How does the Health tab work?

Sobriety Score reads data from Apple Health (with your permission) to show how your body is recovering. This includes sleep, heart rate, HRV, and activity data. All processing happens on your device — nothing is uploaded anywhere.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases." This will check with the App Store and restore your premium access if you've previously purchased.

Is my data backed up?

All your data is stored locally on your device and included in your iPhone's iCloud backup (if enabled). If you restore from an iCloud backup, your Sobriety Score data will be restored too.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request one.

Crisis Resources

If you or someone you know is in crisis, please reach out to one of these resources immediately.
  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988 (US) — available 24/7
  • SAMHSA National Helpline: 1-800-662-4357 — free, confidential, 24/7
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Emergency: Call 911
