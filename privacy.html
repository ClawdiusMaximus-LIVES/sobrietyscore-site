1. Overview

Sobriety Score ("the App") is a sobriety tracking and health recovery app developed by DreamSeeds ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your recovery journey personal.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Sobriety Score collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information. The short version: almost everything stays on your device and we never sell your data to anyone.

2. Information We Collect

2a. Information You Provide

When you use Sobriety Score, you may provide the following information directly:

  • Sobriety date and tracked habits — the substances or habits you are tracking and your start date
  • Journal entries — personal reflections you write within the app
  • Craving logs — timestamps and notes when you log a craving
  • Savings and spending data — amounts you enter for the savings calculator
  • App settings and preferences — your chosen theme, notification preferences, and widget configuration
All of the above data is stored locally on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

2b. Information Collected Automatically

Apple's StoreKit framework processes all purchases. We receive confirmation of purchase status (lifetime unlock) but never see your payment details.

We do not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or paywall SDKs. All purchase flows are handled natively through Apple's StoreKit 2.

3. Apple Health Data

This section is important. Sobriety Score integrates with Apple Health (HealthKit) to help you see how recovery is improving your physical health.

Sobriety Score may request read access to the following Apple Health data types:

  • Sleep Analysis
  • Heart Rate
  • Resting Heart Rate
  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV)
  • Step Count
  • Active Energy Burned

How we handle your health data:

  • All health data remains on your device. We do not transmit, upload, store, or process your health data on any external server.
  • Health data is used solely to display recovery trends within the app (e.g., "Your sleep improved since Day 30").
  • Your health data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising purposes.
  • Your health data is never used for marketing, data mining, or any purpose other than providing the app's core health tracking features to you.
  • Health data is processed entirely on-device using Apple's frameworks.

On-device AI processing: Sobriety Score uses Apple's on-device AI capabilities to provide personalized reflection prompts and insights. All AI processing happens locally on your iPhone. No health data, journal entries, or personal information is sent to any server for AI processing.

You can revoke Sobriety Score's access to Apple Health at any time by going to Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Sobriety Score and disabling any or all data types.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

  • To display your sobriety streak, milestones, and savings calculations
  • To show health recovery trends from Apple Health data
  • To provide AI-powered reflection prompts based on your progress
  • To deliver craving tracking and trend analysis
  • To manage your subscription status and premium feature access
  • To present the in-app purchase offer for premium features

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

5. Data Storage & Security

Sobriety Score is designed as a privacy-first, on-device application.

  • No cloud servers: Your journal entries, sobriety data, health metrics, and craving logs are stored in the app's local data container on your iPhone. We do not operate backend servers that store your personal data.
  • Device encryption: Your data is protected by your iPhone's built-in encryption (Data Protection). When your device is locked, your data is encrypted at rest.
  • No account required: Sobriety Score does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or sign in. There is no user database.
  • Deletion: If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device.

6. Third-Party Services

Sobriety Score integrates with the following third-party services:

Apple HealthKit

Used to read health metrics for the recovery dashboard. Health data stays on-device. See Section 3 above for full details.

Apple's privacy policy: apple.com/privacy

Apple StoreKit 2

Used to process in-app purchases and subscriptions. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We never receive or store your payment information (credit card, Apple ID password, etc.).

Apple On-Device AI (Foundation Models)

Used for AI-powered reflection prompts. All processing occurs on your device using Apple's on-device models. No data is sent to external AI services.

7. Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data in Sobriety Score:

  • Access your data: All your data is visible within the app at all times.
  • Delete your data: Delete the app to remove all data, or use in-app settings to clear specific data (journal entries, craving logs, etc.).
  • Revoke HealthKit access: Go to Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Sobriety Score.
  • Manage subscriptions: Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions.
  • Disable notifications: Go to Settings → Notifications → Sobriety Score.

California Residents (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. Sobriety Score does not sell personal information. Since all data is stored on-device, there is no personal data held on our servers to disclose or delete.

European Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights including access, rectification, erasure, and data portability. Since Sobriety Score stores all data locally on your device and does not transmit personal data to our servers, these rights are exercised directly through your device (by accessing or deleting the app).

8. Children's Privacy

Sobriety Score is intended for adults aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 17. The app deals with substance use recovery, which is not appropriate for minors. If we learn that a user is under 17, we recommend they discontinue use of the app.

9. Subscriptions & Payments

Sobriety Score offers optional premium features through in-app purchases managed entirely by Apple's App Store:

  • All payments are processed by Apple. We never receive, store, or have access to your credit card number, bank account, or Apple ID credentials.
  • Sobriety Score currently offers a one-time purchase to unlock all premium features forever. See the app for current pricing.
  • No recurring subscriptions are used at this time.
  • Refunds are handled by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page and may notify you through the app. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
